The Ministry of Education offers the Bursary to help less fortunate students who cannot afford user fees access education and skills training. Consequently, Kenya as a nation following the creation of the county governments put aside a specific amount of money in their budgets to assist the impoverished and still talented kids to enter education through the bursary initiatives. In this scheme, the needy and gifted students apply for bursaries, which the county government then distributes to their school accounts. This makes it possible for kids in need to obtain education even in difficult circumstances.
